About AiDiTech

Research Focus

The AiDiTech study center focuses on several key research themes, namely :

  • Deep Learning : For the identification of medical digital imagery.
  • Optimization & Tuning : Methods to ensure maximum AI performance.
  • Smartphone Applications : Integration of models into mobile applications.
  • Entrepreneurship : AI-Based technopreneurship.
Facilities & Funding

Equipped with studio facilities and PCs featuring RTX 3600 GPUs. Supported by internal funding from Ma Chung University and external grants from DRTPM.
