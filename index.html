Welcome to AiDitech Research Center

Our research center focuses on the development of Artificial Intelligence (AI), specifically in the field of machine learning and its application for digital image classification. We are committed to producing technological innovations that benefit both society and the industry.



Main Research Theme

Dedicated to advancing AI through rigorous research in medical deep learning and model optimization, ultimately translating technical breakthroughs into impactful mobile applications and technopreneurship.

Deep Learning

Focusing on digital image identification in the healthcare and medical fields, such as the detection of malaria parasites and breast cancer.

Optimization & Tuning

Research on hyperparameter tuning and regularization methods to ensure AI models perform optimally according to the data used.

Technopreneurship

The implementation of machine learning into mobile applications and the development of future technology-based entrepreneurship.
